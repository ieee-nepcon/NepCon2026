About NEPCon 2026

IEEE NEPCon 2026 brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to discuss the latest innovations and research results in next-generation pervasive computing, communication systems, networking architectures, and cybersecurity.

The conference aims to foster collaboration between academia, industry, and government organizations in shaping the future of secure and intelligent connected systems.

Conference Tracks

  • Track 1: Artificial Intelligence / Machine Learning / Deep Learning
  • Track 2: Cybersecurity, Privacy and Trust
  • Track 3: NextG Wireless Systems, Spectrum & Advanced Radio Technologies
  • Track 4: Smart Cities, Smart Mobility, Robotics & Autonomous Platforms
  • Track 5: Quantum Technologies & Post-Classical Systems
  • Track 6: Cyber-Physical Systems and Internet of Things
  • Track 7: Intelligent Edge, Fog & Cloud Continuum
  • Track 8: Data Science, Intelligence, Analytics & System Optimization
  • Track 9: Software-Defined Systems & Virtualized Infrastructures
  • Track 10: Sustainable & Green Digital Systems
  • Track 11: Resilient Systems & Disaster-Aware Technologies
  • Track 12: Human-Centric Pervasive Computing & Extended Reality
  • Track 13: Bio-Inspired Systems
  • Track 14: Distributed Systems

Visa & Travel Information

Visa Requirement:
Nepalese visa is required for all nationalities except Indian nationals. Indian nationals must present a valid passport or voter’s card.

Visa on Arrival

Visa can be obtained “On Arrival” except for citizens of: Nigeria, Ghana, Zimbabwe, Swaziland, Cameroon, Somalia, Liberia, Ethiopia, Iraq, Palestine, Afghanistan, Syria, and refugees with travel documents.

Visa Fees

  • 15 Days – USD 30
  • 30 Days – USD 50
  • 90 Days – USD 125
