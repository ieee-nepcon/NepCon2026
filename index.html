About NEPCon 2026

IEEE NEPCon 2026 brings together researchers, practitioners, and industry experts to discuss the latest innovations and research results in next-generation pervasive computing, communication systems, networking architectures, and cybersecurity.

The conference aims to foster collaboration between academia, industry, and government organizations in shaping the future of secure and intelligent connected systems.
