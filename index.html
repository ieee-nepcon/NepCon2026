Visa & Travel Information

Visa Requirement:
Nepalese visa is required for all nationalities except Indian nationals. Indian nationals must present a valid passport or voter’s card.

Visa on Arrival

Nepalese visa can be obtained “On Arrival” except for citizens of the following countries, who must obtain a visa from a Nepalese diplomatic mission prior to travel:

  • Nigeria
  • Ghana
  • Zimbabwe
  • Swaziland
  • Cameroon
  • Somalia
  • Liberia
  • Ethiopia
  • Iraq
  • Palestine
  • Afghanistan
  • Syria
  • Refugees with travel document

“On Arrival” visas are Tourist Visas with multiple re-entry facility.

Tourist Visa On-Arrival Procedure (TIA – Kathmandu)

  1. Fill out the online form before arrival:
    https://nepaliport.immigration.gov.np/
    (Valid within 15 days of submission.)
  2. Print the barcode receipt or keep a photo on your phone. If not completed online, use kiosk machines at the airport.
  3. Pay visa fee at the airport counter:
    • 15 Days – USD 30
    • 30 Days – USD 50
    • 90 Days – USD 125
    Cash is recommended.
  4. Proceed to Immigration Desk with passport, form, and payment receipt.

Children below 10 years (except US citizens) are exempt from visa fees. SAARC nationals entering Nepal for the first time are exempt from fees for up to 30 days (application still required).

Official information: https://immigration.gov.np/en/page/tourist-visa

Travel to Kathmandu

Tribhuvan International Airport (TIA – KTM) is Nepal’s main international gateway.

Direct connections exist from major hubs including: Tokyo, Seoul, Hong Kong, Kuala Lumpur, Singapore, Bangkok, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Doha, Istanbul, Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Colombo.

Travelers from:
• Eastern US/Canada/Europe – Connect via Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Doha, Istanbul, Delhi
• Western US/Canada/Asia Pacific – Connect via Tokyo, Seoul, Hong Kong, Singapore, Bangkok, Delhi

Conference Hotel

TBD is the official conference venue. All hotels are located in the Thamel tourist district, approximately 6 km (20–30 minutes) from TIA.

Currency & Payments

Local currency: Nepalese Rupee (NPR). Major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) are widely accepted in hotels and restaurants. Please retain foreign exchange receipts.

Electricity

220–240V, 50Hz. Continental plug types. Multi-voltage device users will require a plug adapter.

Climate

October temperature range: 13–27°C (55–81°F). Light daytime clothing with jacket/sweater recommended.

Local Transportation

Metered taxis, EV tempos, buses, and ride-share apps (Pathao, In-Drive) are available. A Nepalese SIM card is required for ride-share apps.

Language

Nepali is the official language. English is widely spoken in Kathmandu.

Food & Beverage

Kathmandu offers traditional Nepali cuisine and international dining options. Mineral water is recommended for drinking.
